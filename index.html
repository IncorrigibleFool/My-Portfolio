Conquest Chess

Conquest Chess is an online chess matchmaking platform built from the ground up in React JS. Games and chat are interactive in real-time due to integration with Socket.io. Games feature extensive chess logic (including advanced moves such as castling) and move help highlighting. A drag-and-drop interface is supported in game, though pieces can be moved via clicking if desired. Score statistics are tracked and are viewable through the main menu.

Cell Sheets

Cell Sheets was a collaborative effort between myself and three other developers. It is an online organizational tool for laboratory research inspired by real industry demand. It features intuitive menuing and design to easily track samples in a large depth of containers. An individual box view allows for easy creation and allocation of samples with a drag-and-drop interface. Individual samples can be selected and tracked and Cell Sheets boasts and impressive, dynamic search system. Cell Sheets also allows foreign integration with temperature sensors to track container temperatures and can issue warnings based on the received data.
